# Product Guide: LeMon (Tipfy)

## Overview
LeMon is a decentralized donation and live-stream overlay platform built on the Monad network. It empowers creators to accept crypto tips with near-instant settlement and real-time visual alerts.

## Target Audience
- **Creators:** Streamers and content creators seeking decentralized, censorship-resistant monetization.
- **Donors/Fans:** Supporters who want to reward creators directly using the Monad ecosystem.
- **Integrators/DAOs:** Organizations looking for robust tipping infrastructure.

## Key Features
- **Web3 Tipping:** Seamless, low-fee crypto donations directly on the Monad network.
- **Live Overlays:** Real-time interactive widgets (Alerts, Goals) that respond to donation events.
- **Dashboard:** A comprehensive "Command Center" for creators to manage settings, view history, and monitor stats.
- **Public Profiles:** Customizable landing pages (`/u/username`) for creators to collect donations.

## Visual Identity
- **Cyberpunk Aesthetic:** High-contrast neon-cyan and pink highlights, futuristic typography, and fluid Framer Motion animations.

## Success Metrics
- **Transaction Volume:** Maximizing the total value of donations processed through the platform.
